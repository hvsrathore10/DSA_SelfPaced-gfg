//Properties of Binary Heap : 
1.Heap is a complete binary tree
2.Heap follow parental Properties ie, 
    leftchild = 2i+1,rightchild = 2i+2, and parent = floor(i-1/2)
3.Types of Heap :- Min Heap and Max Heap
4.Height of Heap : O(㏒N)
5.Maximum possible nodes at any level with Height(h) = N/2^h+1
